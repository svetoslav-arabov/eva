📡 Radio Telescope Project

Building a working radio telescope and documenting the journey.

Project Goal

This project aims to design and build a functional radio telescope that can be used as a practical learning tool for young astronomers who otherwise do not have access to such equipment. It is intended to support education, curiosity, and hands-on experience in astronomy and science. The project also seeks to demonstrate that anyone can achieve meaningful results with determination and hard work. All expenses are covered personally through scholarship awards earned in academic and science competitions.
